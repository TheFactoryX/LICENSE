Introduction

In the world of software licensing, there are many choices: MIT, GPL, Apache, BSD. They all share one thing in common — they assume you need permission.

The Anti-License takes a different approach. It assumes you don't need permission. It assumes you're an adult who can make your own decisions. It assumes that art — including code — should flow freely.

This is the official license of TheFactoryX. All our projects use it. You can use it too.

FAQ

Can I use Anti-Licensed code in commercial projects?

Yes. You can use it anywhere, for anything, including commercial projects. We don't care.

Do I need to include the license in my project?

No. You don't need to include anything. You don't even need to mention us. Do what you want.

Can I modify the Anti-License itself?

Yes. Take it, change it, make it yours. That's the point.

Is this legally valid?

We're artists, not lawyers. If you need legal certainty, consult a lawyer. If you need permission, you're thinking too much.

Why create another license?

Because most licenses are about restriction. This one is about freedom. It's an invitation, not a contract.
